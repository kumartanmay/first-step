Kumar Tanmay

Founder and CEO of Inkredo

I am Kumar Tanmay from New Delhi. Have been residing here since 2007. I participated in cohort 11. I grew up in Dalhousie, Burnpur, and Ranchi. I am having fun with startups since 2008. I am still trying. I am fascinated by the flows of money and its behaviour with changing income groups.

Shifting to writing, I used to maintain a personal diary during my childhood days but abandoned it just before college because it became too sensitive to handle - usual teenage tantrums and parents :-). I took up writing diary again in late 2019 and the pandemic gave me enough room to express myself. I am trying to be a good writer in my own eyes. I want to write the deepest of my thoughts in simple and short sentences. Often my thoughts are so broken that I find it funny when I read them later. Penning down on paper doesn't give me the luxury of editing my thoughts and rearranging them.

I love reading non-fiction, especially business memoirs, biographies, psychology and philosophy.
